ABOUT IJATCE:

AIM & SCOPE: International Journal of Advance Trends in Civil Engineering (IJATCE) (Journal ID: 1CV) is a journal encouraging Civil Engineer, Research Scholar, Practitioner and Students to spot light on the new materials and advance trends in the Civil engineering, to explore the knowledge in near future and get recognized.

Issue: Feb, May, Aug, Nov

Topic of Interest

You can send your manuscript in the following topics but not limited to

  • Structral Engineering
  • civil Engineering

Why IJATCE:

  • Quick response for the submitted Manuscript
  • Maximum of four authors is permitted for each research Manuscript
  • Highly Competent Editorial Board/ Advisory Board Members
  • Quality manuscript selection for originality
  • Peer Reviewed open access of all the articles
  • Very nominal publication charge
  • Good Impact Value from all over the Globe.

Publication Charges

Publishing a manuscript in IJATCE requires manuscript processing charges that will be paid by the submitting author after acceptance of the manuscript for publication in accord with the following standards:

For Indian Authors - INR 2500
For Foreign Authors : USD 90
Charges includes checking of originality, online manuscript publication, soft copy of certificates, posting charges, indexing, maintenance of link resolvers and journal infrastructures.
Payment of Processing Charges
Authors can deposit the publication charges using one of the following options:
  • Credit Cards
  • Debit Cards
  • Net Banking
  • NEFT Transfer
  • Direct Deposit
    • Authors can deposit the publication charges directly to the given account in acceptance mail, by going nearby bank.
