PREPARE MANUSCRIPT

Following are the general guidelines
  • Paper should contain sufficient new materials, with keywords.
  • Scope and Objectives are to be highlighted
  • Presented concisely as per journal standards.
  • Experimental techniques and methods are to be explained.
  • Results should be achieved and are formulated.
  • Conclusions to be precised as per the data and results.
  • All references are to be clearly cited.

Following are the Manuscript guidelines
  • Manuscript should have maximum of 15 pages.
  • Manuscript should be free from plagiarism [ i.e., Manuscript should contain originality]
  • Manuscript must be written in English language using MS-Word (.doc or .docx format only)
  • Maximum numbers of author are Four.
  • Authors are required to sign the Copy right form once their manuscript is accepted, for further publishing process

REVIEW PROCESS OF MANUSCRIPT

  • Manuscript is PEER reviewed.
  • Manuscript is checked for originality.
  • Manuscript is sent to editor in chief for approval and is asked to revert back with comments and suggestions (if needed) for the further publication.
  • Acceptance letter is sent to the author.
  • Online manuscript is published within 7 days from the acceptance of the manuscript.
  • Hard copies of the manuscript and Certificates are provided on demand with the nominal charges as per the journal standards.
